  .. group-tab:: SysV init

    Choose one option according to your operating system:

    a) RPM-based operating system:

      .. code-block:: console

        # chkconfig --add kibana
        # service kibana start
    
    b) Debian-based operating system:

      .. code-block:: console

        # update-rc.d kibana defaults 95 10
        # service kibana start
