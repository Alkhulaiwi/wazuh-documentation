  .. group-tab:: SysV init

    Choose one option according to your operating system.

    a) RPM-based operating systems:

      .. code-block:: console

        # chkconfig wazuh-agent off
        # chkconfig --del wazuh-agent

    b) Debian-based operating systems:

      .. code-block:: console

        # update-rc.d -f wazuh-agent remove
